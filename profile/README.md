# 🍽️ Ordex - Making Online Delivery Accessible

> Empowering drivers, supermarkets, and restaurants with seamless online ordering in Egypt

## 🎯 Our Mission

Ordex is a mobile-first delivery platform built to give **small supermarkets, local restaurants, and independent drivers** a real chance at the online marketplace. We believe that every business should have access to digital commerce, regardless of size.

## 🌟 What We Do

Ordex connects customers with food and groceries from local supermarkets and restaurants through an intuitive mobile app. But we're different—we're built **for** small businesses and gig workers, not against them.

### For Customers
📱 Order food and groceries from your favorite local spots
⚡ Fast, reliable delivery to your door
💳 Secure, flexible payment options

### For Drivers
🚗 Simple, one-tap setup—no complicated onboarding
💰 Transparent earnings with competitive rates
📊 Real-time tracking and order management
🎓 Perfect for students with flexible hours

### For Small Supermarkets & Restaurants
🛍️ Easy vendor dashboard—get online in minutes
📈 Access to more customers instantly
💡 No hidden fees or unfair commission structures
🤝 Fair partnership model that helps you grow
